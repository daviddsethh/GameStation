Destiny 2: A Game in the Future

Destiny 2 is about a world where a "God" named the "Traveler", gives power to soldiers called "Guardians" to face enemies and save the Traveler itself. You have a companion called a "ghost" which floats around you and can revive you anytime you are downed. You also have abilities and can charge up to use a powerful move, called a "Super".

RuneScape: A Game in Medievil Times

RuneScape is a game set in the medievil times where dragons, magic, and knights are. You have 28 different skills to level up and hundreds of quests to do. It is an open world type game, where you have this huge map to explore.

Halo 5: A Fast Paced FPS Game

Halo 5 is close to Destiny 2, as it is a game set in futuristic times. You play as an enhanced soldier known as "Spartans" and you fight aliens that are trying to destroy the world. Since being enhanced you are about 10ft tall and are super strong/fast and are incredibly hard to defeat.
